DETROIT RDH THEATER

Learn the beginnings of Detroit RDH Theater, long form improv. Students will learn how to create scenes based off of audience suggestions. They will learn the fundamentals of 'Yes, And,' how to support scene partners' ideas and energy, and how to make strong character choices. Absolutely no experience in acting or comedy is needed. The Push Comedy Theater strides to create a fun, safe and supportive experience for all students. At the end of the session, students will take part in a graduation show on the Push Comedy Theater stage.
